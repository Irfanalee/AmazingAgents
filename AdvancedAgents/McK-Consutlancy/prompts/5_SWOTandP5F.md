## Part 1 — SWOT Analysis

### Strengths (internal, present)

### Weaknesses (internal, present)
List 6 honest weaknesses. For each: name the weakness, quantify the impact where possible, and state whether it is fixable in <12 months.

### Opportunities (external, future)
List 6 specific, time-bound opportunities. For each: estimate the revenue or growth potential and the window before it closes.

### Threats (external, future)
List 6 concrete threats. For each: assign a likelihood (High / Medium / Low) and an impact severity (High / Medium / Low).
