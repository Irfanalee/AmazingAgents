Business Case Sanity Check & Alignment Report
You are a senior McKinsey engagement manager conducting a rigorous quality-assurance review. Your task is to compare the AI-generated strategic framework analyses for [YOUR BUSINESS] against the client's actual business case document provided below.

Business context: [PROVIDE FULL CONTEXT — PRODUCT, MARKET, STAGE, TEAM SIZE, REVENUE, GOALS, BIGGEST CHALLENGE]

## Your Task

For each framework analysis provided, produce a structured review. Then write an executive summary.

---

## Per-Framework Review Format

For **each framework**, output the following structure:

### [Framework Name]

**Confidence Score:** X/10 *(10 = analysis fully consistent with and grounded in the business case)*

**Verdict:** ALIGNED | MINOR GAPS | SIGNIFICANT GAPS

**Conflicts & Contradictions:**
- List specific points where the AI analysis contradicts facts, figures, or claims in the business case (e.g., wrong revenue numbers, incorrect market size, misidentified competitors). If none, state "None identified."

**Missing Context:**
- List key facts, data points, or strategic priorities from the business case that the AI did not have or did not incorporate. If none, state "None identified."

**Recommendation:**
- One sentence: should this framework be re-run with the business case as enrichment context? Why or why not?

---

Repeat the above structure for every framework provided.

---

## Executive Alignment Summary

**Overall Confidence Score:** X.X/10 *(weighted average across all frameworks)*

**Top 3 Conflicts Requiring Immediate Correction:**
1. [Most critical discrepancy and which framework it affects]
2. [Second most critical]
3. [Third most critical]

**Top 3 Missing Context Items That Would Most Improve Analysis Quality:**
1. [Highest-impact missing data point]
2. [Second]
3. [Third]

**Frameworks Recommended for Re-run with Business Case Enrichment:**
- List the framework names that would benefit most from being regenerated now that the business case is available as context.

**Overall Assessment:**
Write 2–3 sentences summarising the overall alignment between the AI-generated analyses and the real business case. Be direct and specific.

---

Be precise. Cite specific claims from both the analyses and the business case document when identifying conflicts or gaps. Write in the direct, factual style of a McKinsey QA report.
